About Alganize

A browser-based explorer for differential gene expression across microalgae, plants, and soil microbiome stress-response studies. (Work in progress.)

Gene-expression patterns

This app focuses on differential gene expression in microalgae and plant-related metabolite pathways — such as carotenogenesis, fatty acid biosynthesis, and osmolyte production — across stress conditions like salt or high-light treatment. For each comparison (contrast), the displayed log₂ fold-change values show how strongly individual genes are up- or down-regulated relative to control, highlighting pathways that are transcriptionally co-activated or repressed under a given stimulus.
