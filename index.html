About Metabolite Gene Explorer

(Work in progress)

Gene‑expression patterns
This browser app focuses on differential gene expression in microalgae and plant‑related
metabolite pathways, such as carotenogenesis, fatty acid biosynthesis, and osmolyte 
production, across stress conditions (e.g., salt or high‑light treatment). For each 
comparison (contrast), the displayed log₂ fold‑change values show how strongly individual 
genes are up‑ or down‑regulated relative to control, highlighting pathways that are 
transcriptionally co‑activated or re‑pressed under a given stimulus.
